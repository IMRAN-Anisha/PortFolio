My "WHY?"

I believe technology is a tool for empowerment. Whether it's building smart tools or supporting students through tutoring, I’m driven by a passion to help others grow with me. From the first lines of code written in quiet curiosity on scratch, to apps crafted with purpose & passion, my journey has been one of growth and discovery. Each project is a steptoward a future where technology not only solves problems but opens doors turning ideas into impact.

My Journey

I was born and raised in Perth, where I built the foundation of who I am—curious, driven, and always eager to learn. Moving to Sydney for university marked a huge transition in my life: a new city, new people, and a whole new level of independence. The last two years of highschool came with significant challenges—personal setbacks, academic pressure, and moments where giving up felt easier. But I made a choice: to stay dedicated. Instead of letting struggles define me, I turned them into fuel.

I pushed forward, learned to manage my time, set goals, and stayed grounded in purpose. Through this journey, I realised something powerful: growth feels better when it’s shared. That’s why I now dedicate my time to helping others—whether it’s through tutoring, tech projects, or simply sharing lessons I’ve learned along the way. My path has been anything but linear, and I wouldn’t want it any other way.
